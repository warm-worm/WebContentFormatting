Fascinating Facts About the World

Did You Know About Space?

The largest known star is UY Scuti, which is so big that if it were placed at the center of our solar system, it would extend beyond Jupiter's orbit.

A day on Venus lasts longer than a year on Venus. This is because Venus takes more time to rotate on its axis than to complete one orbit around the Sun.

Surprising Facts About Nature

Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood. Two of their hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body.

Trees can communicate with each other through underground fungal networks, often referred to as the "Wood Wide Web." They can share nutrients and send warnings about diseases or pests.
